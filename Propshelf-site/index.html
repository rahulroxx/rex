About PropShelf design

PropShelf is an online subscription based real estate data and analytics platform. It is a premier Business Intelligence product in the Realty space in India. This app will allow brokers to serve customers better and close deals faster. The platform will keep agents updated about latest inventories and requirements in the realty market.

With PropShelf, you can buy/sell properties, you can post a flat for sale, find an apartment to rent, buy a new property, discover a house to purchase, select a PG/hostel, find the right tenant, and look up new projects.
